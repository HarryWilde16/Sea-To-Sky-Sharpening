Frequently Asked Questions

How long does sharpening take?

Most items take 2-5 business days. We offer same-day service for urgent needs. Contact us for availability!

Can you sharpen any blade?

We sharpen almost any blade including kitchen knives (Western and Japanese), garden tools, hunting knives, axes, and scissors. Contact us about specialty items!

Do you offer discounts?

Yes! We offer discounts for multiple items and special rates for local restaurants and commercial clients. Ask us about volume pricing.

Is mobile service available?

Yes! We offer convenient mobile service in Pemberton and Whistler. Contact us to discuss mobile scheduling for your location.

What if I'm not happy with the sharpness?

We stand behind our work! If you're not satisfied, let us know and we'll re-sharpen your items at no charge.
