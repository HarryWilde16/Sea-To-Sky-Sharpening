Essential Knife Care Tips for Kitchen & Garden Tools

Maintain your blades between professional sharpening visits to Sea to Sky Sharpening

1️⃣

Use a Cutting Board

Always use a wooden or plastic cutting board. Hard surfaces like granite, glass, or marble dull blades rapidly.

2️⃣

Hand Wash Only

Avoid the dishwasher! Hand wash your knives and dry them immediately to prevent damage and corrosion.

3️⃣

Store Safely

Use a knife block, magnetic strip, or blade guards. Loose storage damages edges and is unsafe.

4️⃣

Hone Regularly

Use a honing steel 1–2 times per week to realign the blade's edge between professional sharpenings.

5️⃣
